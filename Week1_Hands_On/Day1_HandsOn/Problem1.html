Bawarchi

Hotel Bawarchi is one of the most famous restaurants in Hyderabad, especially known for its authentic Hyderabadi biryani Located at RTC X Roads, it has been serving delicious food for decades and attracts both locals and tourists. The restaurant is particularly popular for its mutton and chicken biryani, which are rich in aroma, spices, and traditional flavors. Apart from biryani, it also offers a variety of kebabs, curries, and tandoori dishes. Bawarchi is known for its affordable prices and crowded atmosphere, reflecting its strong reputation for taste and quality
